Injection Grade PCTA is designed for high-speed injection molding applications, ideal for complex shapes and detailed designs. Extruded Grade is tailored for extrusion processes, producing films, sheets, or profiles with consistent thickness. Blow Molding Grade is optimized for manufacturing hollow containers, providing excellent strength and clarity. Each grade serves distinct applications, ensuring versatility across industries such as packaging, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
