<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Rotor Locks</li><li>Electric Rotor Locks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotor locks are safety devices used in various machinery to secure rotating elements. The hydraulic rotor locks utilize pressurized fluid to engage and disengage the locking mechanism, offering robust performance in high-load applications. In contrast, electric rotor locks operate using electric motors, providing precise control and faster actuation. Both types are essential in industries like aviation, manufacturing, and energy, ensuring equipment stability during maintenance and preventing accidental rotor movement, thus enhancing safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
