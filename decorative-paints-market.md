<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The decorative paints market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based categories. Solvent-based paints contain volatile organic compounds, offering durable finishes and a wide range of colors, making them suitable for outdoor applications. However, they pose health and environmental concerns. In contrast, water-based paints are eco-friendly, with lower VOC levels, making them safer for indoor use. They dry quickly and offer easy maintenance, but may require more coats for full coverage. Each type caters to specific user preferences and project requirements.</p></p>
