<p><strong>The Fluorescence Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Epifluorescence Microscopy</li><li>Inverted Fluorescence Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluorescence microscope market features several types, including epifluorescence microscopy and inverted fluorescence microscopy. Epifluorescence microscopy illuminates the sample from above, allowing simultaneous capture of emitted light, making it ideal for imaging thin specimens. Inverted fluorescence microscopes, on the other hand, have their light source and objectives positioned below the specimen, facilitating the observation of larger samples or cells in culture dishes. Both types enhance the visualization of fluorescently labeled components in biological and material science research.</p></p>
