<p><p>The high-throughput pipetting market consists of two main types: manual pipetting systems and electric pipetting systems. Manual pipetting involves traditional pipettes operated by hand, offering flexibility and control but requiring more time and effort for large sample volumes. In contrast, electric pipetting systems automate the process, providing enhanced speed, precision, and consistency, making them ideal for high-volume laboratories. The choice between the two systems often depends on the specific needs and workflow of the laboratory environment.</p></p>
