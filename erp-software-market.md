<p><ul><li>On Premise ERP</li><li>Cloud-Based ERP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ERP software market can be categorized into two primary types: on-premise ERP and cloud-based ERP. On-premise ERP involves installing software on the company's own servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based ERP is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs through subscription models. This allows businesses to benefit from automatic updates and easier collaboration, making cloud solutions increasingly popular.</p></p>
