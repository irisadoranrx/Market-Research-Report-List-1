<p><ul><li>Fuel Cell Technology</li><li>Semiconductor Oxide Sensor Technology</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alcohol sensor market comprises various technologies, primarily categorized into fuel cell technology and semiconductor oxide sensor technology, along with other emerging types. Fuel cell sensors utilize electrochemical reactions to accurately detect alcohol levels, offering high sensitivity and specificity, making them popular in breathalyzers. Semiconductor oxide sensors rely on changes in electrical resistance upon alcohol exposure, providing a cost-effective solution but generally with lower precision. Other technologies include infrared sensors and biosensors, which are being developed for more niche applications in alcohol detection.</p></p>
