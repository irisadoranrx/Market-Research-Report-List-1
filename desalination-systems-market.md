<p><p>Desalination systems market comprises various technologies for converting seawater into freshwater. Thermal technology utilizes heat to evaporate water, then condense it for purification, making it effective in large-scale operations but energy-intensive. Membrane technology, including reverse osmosis, employs semi-permeable membranes to separate salt from water, offering energy efficiency and operational flexibility, ideal for diverse applications. Other technologies may include advanced processes like electrodialysis and solar desalination, catering to niche markets and specific needs in water scarcity management.</p></p>
