<p><p>The cable cleats market is categorized into three main types: Single Type, Multicore Type, and Trefoil Type. Single Type cleats secure individual cables, providing flexibility and ease of installation. Multicore Type cleats are designed for bundling multiple cables together, ensuring organized routing and protection. Trefoil Type cleats accommodate three cables arranged in a triangular formation, promoting optimal heat dissipation and minimizing electromagnetic interference. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing safety and performance in electrical installations.</p></p>
