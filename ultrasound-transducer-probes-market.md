<p><p>The ultrasound transducer probes market comprises various types, each tailored for specific applications. Linear type probes provide high-resolution images for superficial structures and are ideal for vascular imaging. Convex type probes offer wider scanning views and are suitable for abdominal examinations. Phased array type probes are compact, enabling cardiac imaging and small spaces. Endocavitary type probes are designed for internal examinations, such as gynecological or rectal assessments. Other types may include specialty probes for unique procedures, expanding diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
