<p><p>Strain gauge pressure sensors are critical in measuring pressure by converting mechanical strain into an electrical signal. The market for these sensors is divided into two main types based on output: analog and digital. Analog output sensors provide a continuous voltage or current signal proportional to the pressure, allowing for real-time monitoring. In contrast, digital output sensors deliver discrete signals, often incorporating microprocessors for enhanced accuracy and data processing capabilities, making them suitable for more complex applications and systems integration.</p></p>
