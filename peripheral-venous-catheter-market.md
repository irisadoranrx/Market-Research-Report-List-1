<p><ul><li>Temporary Catheter</li><li>Short-term Catheter</li><li>Long term Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peripheral Venous Catheter market comprises three main types: temporary, short-term, and long-term catheters. Temporary catheters are used for brief medical procedures, typically lasting a few hours to a couple of days. Short-term catheters are designed for use over several days to a few weeks, often for ongoing treatments like IV fluids or medications. Long-term catheters are intended for extended use, spanning weeks to months, and are utilized for patients with chronic conditions requiring consistent venous access.</p></p>
