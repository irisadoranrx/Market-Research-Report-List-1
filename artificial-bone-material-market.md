<p><ul><li>Inorganic Material</li><li>Organic Material</li><li>Composite Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial bone material market consists of three main types: inorganic, organic, and composite materials. Inorganic materials, such as hydroxyapatite and bioglass, provide structural support and integrate well with bone. Organic materials, including polymers like polycaprolactone, enhance biocompatibility and promote tissue growth. Composite materials combine both inorganic and organic components, offering improved mechanical properties and biological functionality. Each type caters to specific clinical needs, driving advancements in bone regeneration and orthopedic applications.</p></p>
