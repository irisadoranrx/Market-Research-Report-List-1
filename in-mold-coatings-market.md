<p><p>The In-Mold Coatings market comprises three primary types: water-based, solvent-based, and powder-based coatings. Water-based coatings are eco-friendly, offering easier cleanup and lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Solvent-based coatings provide superior durability and finish but generate higher VOC emissions. Powder-based coatings are applied electrostatically and cured under heat, resulting in a tough finish with minimal environmental impact. Each type caters to specific application needs, balancing performance, environmental considerations, and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
