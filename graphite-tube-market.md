<p><p>The Graphite Tube market comprises three primary types: Resin Bonded, Carbon Bonded, and Fully Graphitized graphite tubes. Resin Bonded tubes are created using thermosetting resins, offering good strength and thermal resistance. Carbon Bonded tubes utilize a carbon matrix for enhanced durability and performance, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. Fully Graphitized graphite tubes undergo a process to achieve high purity and electrical conductivity, ideal for specialized industrial applications where superior thermal and electrical properties are essential.</p></p>
