<p><p>The High-Speed Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Market is primarily segmented into two types: 2D and 3D inspection systems. </p><p>**2D AOI** utilizes flat imaging techniques to examine the surface characteristics of components, focusing on defects like misalignment, solder issues, and missing parts. </p><p>Conversely, **3D AOI** employs advanced imaging technology to assess height and volume, providing a more comprehensive analysis of components. This enables the detection of hidden defects and ensures better quality control in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
