<p><ul><li>Open Loop VCM Driver IC</li><li>Closed Loop VCM Driver IC</li><li>OIS VCM Driver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VCM Camera Driver IC market includes three primary types: Open Loop VCM Driver ICs, Closed Loop VCM Driver ICs, and OIS VCM Driver ICs. Open Loop VCMs offer simpler control and lower cost, making them suitable for basic applications. Closed Loop VCMs feature feedback mechanisms for precise positioning, enhancing image quality in mid to high-end devices. OIS VCM Driver ICs specifically cater to Optical Image Stabilization, providing advanced motion compensation for superior stabilization in cameras and smartphones, improving overall image clarity.</p></p>
