<p><p>Dental endodontics is a specialized field of dentistry focused on the diagnosis and treatment of diseases and injuries affecting the dental pulp and surrounding tissues. As oral health awareness grows, the demand for endodontic procedures is rising, contributing significantly to the market's expansion.</p><p>The Dental Endodontics Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.7% during the forecast period.
