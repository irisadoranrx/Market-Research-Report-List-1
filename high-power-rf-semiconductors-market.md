Silicon is widely used for its cost-effectiveness and reliability in lower power applications. GaN offers superior efficiency and power density, making it ideal for high-frequency applications. SiC excels in high-temperature and high-power applications, providing durability and reduced energy losses, making each type suited for specialized industrial applications.</p></p>
