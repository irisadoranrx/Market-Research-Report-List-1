<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>PLC Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horizontal metal baler market is divided into two primary types: manual and PLC automatic. Manual balers require operators to perform tasks like feeding and tying baled materials, offering lower initial costs but higher labor intensity. PLC automatic balers, on the other hand, are equipped with programmable logic control systems, enabling automated operations for efficiency and higher throughput. This automation reduces labor costs and minimizes human error, making it ideal for larger-scale operations in the recycling and waste management sectors.</p></p>
