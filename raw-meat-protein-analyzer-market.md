<p><p>The Raw Meat Protein Analyzer market encompasses distinct technologies such as DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy). DEXA technology provides precise measurement of protein and fat content in meat by utilizing X-ray beams, ensuring accurate compositional analysis. In contrast, NIR technology employs light absorption techniques to quickly assess protein levels based on the interaction of near-infrared light with meat samples. Both technologies play crucial roles in quality control and nutritional assessment within the meat industry.</p></p>
