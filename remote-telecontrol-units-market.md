<p><p>The Remote Telecontrol Units (RTU) market is segmented into two main types: Wireless RTUs and Wired RTUs. Wireless RTUs utilize radio signals, cellular networks, or satellite communications for data transmission, offering flexibility and ease of installation, especially in remote locations. In contrast, Wired RTUs rely on physical connections like Ethernet or serial communication, providing stability and reliability in data transmission. Each type caters to different application needs, such as environmental monitoring, utility management, and industrial automation.</p></p>
