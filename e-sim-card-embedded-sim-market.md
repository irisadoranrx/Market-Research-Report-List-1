<p><ul><li>SMD</li><li>SIP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The eSIM market is categorized primarily into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and SIP (System in Package). SMD eSIMs are compact chips soldered directly onto circuit boards, allowing for high-density integration in devices. SIP eSIMs, on the other hand, encapsulate the eSIM within a package that may contain additional components, enabling more efficient space utilization and improved performance. Both types serve various applications, from smartphones to IoT devices, contributing to the growing trend of embedded connectivity.</p></p>
