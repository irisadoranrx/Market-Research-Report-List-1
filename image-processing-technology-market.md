<p><ul><li>Point Processing</li><li>Group Processing</li><li>Geometry Processing</li><li>Frame Processing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Image processing technology encompasses various market types, including point processing, group processing, geometry processing, and frame processing. Point processing focuses on individual pixel manipulation, enhancing images or adjusting brightness and contrast. Group processing involves modifying a group of pixels simultaneously for tasks like filtering or segmentation. Geometry processing alters the spatial configuration of images, aiding in tasks like rotation and scaling. Frame processing deals with video sequences, analyzing multiple frames to enhance quality or extract information over time, critical for dynamic applications.</p></p>
