<p><ul><li>Preheating Chamber Furnace</li><li>Preheating Tunnel Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The preheating furnace market includes two main types: preheating chamber furnaces and preheating tunnel furnaces. Preheating chamber furnaces are enclosed units that prepare materials by raising their temperature using various heating methods, ensuring uniform heat distribution for processes like welding or casting. In contrast, preheating tunnel furnaces continuously process materials as they pass through a heated tunnel, optimizing efficiency for high-volume production. Both types are essential in industries like metallurgy, automotive, and manufacturing for improving material properties and energy savings.</p></p>
