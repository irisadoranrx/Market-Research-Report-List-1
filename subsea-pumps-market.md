<p><p>The subsea pumps market comprises several types, each designed for specific applications in offshore oil and gas extraction. Helico-axial pumps utilize screw-shaped impellers for efficient fluid movement, while electrical submersible pumps (ESPs) operate underwater to extract fluids effectively. Centrifugal pumps use rotational energy to move fluids, and twin screw pumps feature interlocking screws for smooth operation. Hybrid pumps combine technologies for enhanced performance, while counter-axial pumps are designed for high-capacity applications requiring compact solutions. Each type plays a vital role in subsea operations.</p></p>
